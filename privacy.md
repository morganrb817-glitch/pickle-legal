# Privacy Policy — Pickle: Help Me Decide

**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026

## 1. Who We Are

Pickle ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by Morgan Bennett. You can reach us at iminapickle.app@gmail.com.

## 2. What Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information:** When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address from those providers.
- **Display name and avatar:** You choose a display name and emoji avatar in the app.
- **Phone number:** Optionally provided for contact matching to find friends.
- **Content you post:** Text and photos you submit as Pickle options.
- **Votes:** The choices you make when voting on Pickles.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Push notification token:** To send you notifications about votes, friends, and activity.
- **Usage data:** Which Pickles you view and vote on, timing data used to calculate your Pickle Personality.
- **Device information:** Standard mobile app data including device type and OS version.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Operate the app and provide core features (posting, voting, friend connections)
- Match you with contacts who are also on Pickle (only if you grant contacts permission)
- Send push notifications about your Pickles and friends' activity
- Calculate your Pickle Personality and voter ranking
- Moderate content for safety (text and images reviewed by automated services)
- Respond to support requests

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only with:
- **Supabase** — our database, authentication, and storage provider (supabase.com)
- **Apple / Google** — authentication providers you choose to sign in with
- **OpenAI** — text content is sent to OpenAI's Moderation API for safety screening (no data is used to train OpenAI models)
- **Google Cloud Vision** — images are sent to Google's SafeSearch API for safety screening
- **RevenueCat** — processes subscription purchases (revenuecat.com)
- **Expo** — used for push notification delivery (expo.dev)

**Public vs. Friends content:**
- Public Pickles are anonymous — your name is not shown.
- Friends Pickles show your display name to your friends only.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Delete Account, which permanently removes your profile, Pickles, and votes.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Pickle is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 7. Push Notifications

We send push notifications for friend requests, votes on your Pickles, and new Pickles from friends. You can disable notifications at any time in your device's Settings app.

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data (use Settings → Delete Account, or email us)
- Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at iminapickle.app@gmail.com.

## 9. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Supabase Row Level Security to protect your data. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions.
